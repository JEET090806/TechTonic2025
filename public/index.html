Rules & Regulations

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, please adhere to the following rules.

Team Formation:

Each team must consist of 2 to 5 members, all from the same institute.

Registration Fee Rule:

A registration fee of ₹100 per member must be paid during team registration.

Originality Rule:

All ideas and solutions must be original; plagiarism or duplication will lead to disqualification.

Code of Conduct:

Participants must maintain professional behavior and avoid any form of misconduct.

Technology Usage Rule:

Use of open-source tools, libraries, and APIs is encouraged, but must be acknowledged properly.

Hardware/Software Limitation:

Participants must bring their own laptops, chargers, and other devices required for development.
